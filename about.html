About Us

Inclusive Voice

Inclusive Voice is an AI tool that converts Nepali Sign Language into text and speech. It helps deaf and hearing people communicate more easily.

Our Goal

To bridge communication gaps between the deaf and hearing communities in Nepal using AI-powered sign language recognition.

Our Story

Inclusive Voice started as a final-year capstone project by a group of computer science students who wanted to use technology for social impact. Inspired by the challenges faced by the Nepali deaf community, we developed an AI model that recognizes Nepali Sign Language and converts it into text and speech.

Project Highlights

Real-time Sign Detection

Advanced AI technology that recognizes and interprets Nepali Sign Language gestures in real-time.

Dual Language Output

Seamlessly converts signs into both Nepali and English text for broader accessibility.

Audio Support

Converts text to natural-sounding speech in multiple languages for complete communication.
