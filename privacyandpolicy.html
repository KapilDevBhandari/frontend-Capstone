Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Introduction

At Inclusive Voice, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our digital sign language recognition platform.

We believe that transparency is essential to building trust with our users. This policy outlines our practices in clear, understandable language so you can make informed decisions about your data.

Last updated: December 2024

Data Collection

We collect information to provide and improve our sign language recognition services. The types of data we collect include:

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Profile preferences and settings
  • Device information and browser type

Sign Language Data

  • Video recordings of sign language gestures (processed locally when possible)
  • Recognition accuracy metrics and feedback
  • Usage patterns and interaction data
  • Training data contributions (with explicit consent)

Technical Data

  • IP address and location data (for service optimization)
  • Session duration and feature usage
  • Error logs and performance metrics
  • Cookie and tracking data (with consent)

How Data is Used

We use your data responsibly and only for legitimate purposes that benefit our users and improve our services:

  • Service Improvement
    Enhance sign language recognition accuracy and develop new features based on usage patterns.
  • Personalization
    Customize your experience with personalized recommendations and adaptive learning features.
  • Analytics & Research
    Analyze usage trends to understand user needs and improve accessibility features.
  • Communication
    Send important updates, security notifications, and optional feature announcements.
  • Technical Support
    Provide customer support, troubleshoot issues, and maintain platform security.
  • Legal Compliance
    Meet legal obligations and protect against fraud, abuse, or security threats.

Data Sharing

Our Commitment

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes without your explicit consent.

We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

Trusted third-party services that help us operate our platform (cloud hosting, analytics) under strict data protection agreements.

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

Research Partnerships

Anonymized, aggregated data may be shared with academic institutions for accessibility research (with your consent).

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data would be transferred under the same privacy protections.

Data Security

  • Encryption
    All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access Controls
    Strict access controls and authentication for all team members.
  • Regular Audits
    Periodic security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Data Minimization
    We collect only the data necessary for our services.
